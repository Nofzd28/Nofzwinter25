About the NFL

The organization behind America's greatest game

Our Mission

The mission of the National Football League (NFL) is to provide fans with the highest quality football experience and inspire communities through the power of sport.

Our Vision

To be the premier sports and entertainment organization in the world, promoting excellence, unity, and passion for football on a global scale.

Our Goals

  • Deliver thrilling and competitive games every season.
  • Foster player development, safety, and integrity.
  • Engage communities through outreach and inclusion programs.
  • Continue growing the global influence of American football.

History & Achievements

The NFL was founded in 1920 and has since grown into a cultural phenomenon, captivating millions of fans around the world. With iconic teams, unforgettable games, and legendary athletes, the league has hosted 59 Super Bowls and shaped the history of American sports.

Notable achievements include groundbreaking player safety reforms, global expansion with international games, and charitable initiatives like NFL Play 60 and Inspire Change.

The Legacy Continues

From the first kickoff to Super Bowl LX, the NFL remains at the heart of American sports culture.
